🎯 YouTube Focus

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective immediately
YouTube Focus does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

YouTube Focus is a Chrome browser extension that modifies the YouTube interface based on user-defined profiles. This policy explains what data we access, what we do with it, and what we never do.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. Your profiles, keyword filters, and playlist shortcuts are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.sync API. This syncs across your own Chrome browsers via your existing Google account — we never see or touch this data.

We have no access to:

  • Your YouTube watch history
  • Your Google account information
  • Your browsing history
  • Any personally identifiable information

What We Never Do

  • Analytics or usage tracking
  • Crash reporting sent to external servers
  • Advertising or ad targeting
  • Cookies set by this extension
  • Data sold or shared with third parties
  • External servers or databases of any kind

Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

Permission Why It's Needed
storage Saves your profiles and settings locally on your device
tabs Reloads YouTube tabs when you switch profiles
scripting Injects the focus UI and blocking rules into YouTube pages
host_permissions (youtube.com) Required to modify the YouTube interface

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit any data.

Third Party Services

YouTube Focus does not integrate with any third party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The extension operates entirely within your browser and only communicates with YouTube.com as a normal part of browsing.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
