# Privacy Policy — YouTube Focus

**Last updated:** April 2026  
**Contact:** saloni2409@gmail.com

---

## Overview

YouTube Focus is a Chrome browser extension that modifies the YouTube interface based on user-defined profiles. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains clearly what data we do and do not collect.

---

## Data We Collect

**YouTube Focus does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

All settings, profiles, keyword filters, and playlist shortcuts are stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in `chrome.storage.sync` API. This data syncs across your own Chrome browsers via your Google account but is never accessed by us.

We do not have access to:
- Your YouTube watch history
- Your Google account information
- Your browsing history
- Any personally identifiable information

---

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ No analytics or usage tracking
- ❌ No crash reporting sent to external servers
- ❌ No advertising identifiers
- ❌ No cookies set by this extension
- ❌ No data sold or shared with third parties
- ❌ No external servers or databases — ever

---

## Permissions Explained

The extension requests the following Chrome permissions:

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|---|---|
| `storage` | Saves your profiles and settings locally on your device |
| `tabs` | Reloads YouTube tabs when you switch profiles |
| `scripting` | Injects the focus UI and blocking rules into YouTube pages |
| `host_permissions` (youtube.com) | Required to modify the YouTube interface |

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data.

---

## Third Party Services

YouTube Focus does not integrate with any third party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

The extension operates entirely within your browser and only communicates with YouTube.com as a normal part of browsing.

---

## Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
